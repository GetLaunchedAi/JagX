About JagX Security & Investigations

JagX Security & Investigations is a Michigan-based security and investigations firm led by Mo Kazan, a seasoned federal law enforcement professional with over 20 years of service in the Department of Homeland Security, Department of Defense, and the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs.

Our team is made up of veterans, former law enforcement, and highly trained security guards. With proven success in personal protection, private estate security, background checks, asset location, and surveillance, we deliver peace of mind with professionalism and discretion.

At JagX, our mission is simple: provide trusted, professional, and reliable protection. We pride ourselves on competitive pricing, consistent client relationships, and a team with extensive training and real-world experience. Whether you need armed security, investigative services, or intelligence consulting, we are here to safeguard what matters most.
